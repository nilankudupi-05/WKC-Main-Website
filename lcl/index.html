Why LCL

Built for the youngest creators

Ages 3–5 are the most formative years. LCL gives every young child a safe, joyful first experience of being celebrated for what they love.

🌱
Age-Appropriate Everything

Activities, judging criteria and expectations are designed specifically for children aged 3–5 — no adult standards applied to young minds.

👨‍🏫
Expert-Guided

Every submission is reviewed by WKC's trained faculty — and every child receives warm, encouraging written feedback, not just a score.

🏆
Every Child Is Celebrated

There are no losers in LCL. Participation certificates go to every child. Because at age 3–5, trying is the achievement.

The Journey

How LCL Works

Three simple steps — from home to celebrated champion.

01
Register Free & Pick a Category

Choose the activity your child loves most. Registration is free and takes two minutes.

02
Submit at Home

Record a short video or photograph your child's work at home — no venue, no pressure. Submit through the WKC portal.

03
Every Child Gets Celebrated

Our faculty reviews every entry with care and warmth. Every child receives a personalised certificate and written encouragement — not a ranking.

The LCL Promise:
No child leaves without a reason to smile.

We don't hand out consolation prizes. We recognise every young child's courage to try — because that is the real achievement at age 3–5.
