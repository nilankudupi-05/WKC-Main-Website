Our Story

Built to Hero your child.

WizKidsCarnival started with a simple belief — every child has a talent worth celebrating. We built the stage. Now 500,000+ kids across India and 10 countries call it theirs.

Our Mission

One mission. Unwavering.

“WKC is committed to a single mission — to Hero your child and to celebrate your child's talents and skills.”

We give children a global platform to showcase both their academic and creative abilities — while developing the confidence that comes from doing what they love. Our vision is a world where every child feels empowered to display their abilities, enjoys challenges, and grows into a confident, well-rounded individual capable of taking on the world.

The People Behind WKC

Meet the Founders

Three parents who wanted more for their children — and built a platform for millions.

M
Mansi
Co-Founder

A former Product Manager at Novartis and IBM, Mansi co-founded WKC from a deeply personal place — her own shyness as a child, and her determination that her daughter would grow up with the confidence she didn't have. She discovered that structured competition and performance were the most effective tools for building that confidence, and built WKC around that insight.

NovartisIBMProduct Management
A
Abhishek
Co-Founder

A CA and MBA graduate from the Indian School of Business, Abhishek spent years at Morgan Stanley before pivoting to education technology. He co-founded makkajai — an educational gaming startup — before joining Mansi and Vinayak to build WizKidsCarnival. He leads the business and growth vision.
