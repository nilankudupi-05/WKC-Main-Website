The Journey

How It Works

From registration to grand finale — a step-by-step journey to the national stage.

01
Register & Choose Your Category

Register free and pick the category your child loves. Submit at ₹250 + GST per contest. WKC subscribers participate free.

02
Level 1 & Level 2 — Online Rounds

Two qualifying rounds run July–November. Submit entries online — videos, writing or audio depending on category. Expert judges score and shortlist.

03
Semi-Finals — Physical Event

Top performers from Level 2 qualify for physical semi-finals. Free entry for all qualifiers. Held across major cities.

04
Grand Finale — Pune

The national final. January–February. The country's best young talent compete for the KPL title, ₹2 Lakh in prizes, and a magazine feature.
